================================================================================
1. PURPOSE AND BOUNDARY
================================================================================

Quizzes let users test and build massage, spa, and wellness knowledge in a light, rewarding
format. A quiz item is a platform-authored question with defined choices, a correct answer, and
a short explanation shown after answering.

Casual quizzes (such as the homepage widget) are engagement and learning features. They are not
assessments, certifications, or credentials, and a quiz result must never be presented as a
professional qualification. Formal course assessment belongs to the Massage Campus specification.

================================================================================
2. PLACEMENT
================================================================================

The current placement is the homepage right sidebar (Campus Quiz). Quiz items may later appear
on other approved pages and, when the Campus module ships, inside Campus lessons and courses.
Placement does not change ownership of the quiz record or its results.

================================================================================
3. PARTICIPATION AND COMPLETION ROTATION
================================================================================

Participation:
- registered users participate normally, and their quiz results (item, answer given,
  correctness, time) are recorded per user;
- guests may answer casually; guest results are not recorded as user history, and guests may be
  invited to sign in for saved progress;
- one user is scored on a given quiz item once; repeat attempts, where allowed, must not farm
  additional rewards for the same item.

After answering, the widget shows whether the answer was correct and a short explanation.

Completion rotation (accepted direction):
- after the result and explanation are shown, and after a short delay, a new unanswered quiz
  item replaces the completed one in place if the user is still on the page;
- rotation is per user and completion-aware: any page showing the quiz widget serves items the
  user has not yet answered, not items they already completed;
- when no unanswered items remain for the user, the widget may show a neutral message, recent
  performance, or hide, rather than repeating completed items;
- completion-aware rotation requires an identified user; guests may receive simple time-based
  rotation instead.

Replacing the completed question in place is a dynamic update and must follow Accessibility and
Inclusive Design Requirements: the correctness result, explanation, and arrival of a new
question are announced to assistive technology (aria-live), and keyboard focus is not lost or
hijacked by the swap.

================================================================================
4. RESULTS, REWARDS, AND CAMPUS RELATIONSHIP
================================================================================

- user quiz results are recorded per user and visible to that user as personal history;
- quiz results are private by default and are not public profile content;
- quiz completion may earn rewards per the Reward System (Campus quiz completion is a possible
  Nexus Points and Inklings source), subject to its caps and
  anti-farming rules;
- correctness may gate rewards or reward size; that detail belongs to the Reward System.

Open decision — Campus integration: whether casual quiz results (homepage and general-page
quizzes) are stored together with, feed into, or remain fully separate from formal Massage
Campus learner progress, course assessment, and knowledge-check records is not yet decided.
Until decided, casual quiz results and Campus assessment records must not be silently merged,
and nothing in this document creates a Campus credential or completion effect.

================================================================================
5. ADMINISTRATION AND STORAGE
================================================================================

Quiz administration (draft, question and response structure, audience, availability window,
translation, accessibility, moderation, scoring rules, preview, publication, closure, export
authorization, archive) belongs to the Admin Workspace editorial area per
docs/11-workspaces/admin-workspace.txt.

Final quiz and result collections, field names, and option codes are deferred and will be
defined through the database-structure and structure-guide process before implementation. The
current homepage Campus Quiz widget is a visual preview; progress is not stored yet.

================================================================================
6. OPEN DECISIONS
================================================================================

- relationship between casual quiz results and formal Campus learner progress (see section 4);
- final quiz and result collection design;
- repeat-attempt policy and whether later correct answers change recorded history;
- difficulty levels, streaks, or leaderboard-style presentations and their privacy rules;
- whether quiz items are versioned when a question or correct answer is corrected.
