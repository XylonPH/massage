================================================================================
1. MEANING
================================================================================

Inklings are the second Massage Nexus gamification currency. The earlier working name was Ink Drops;
the accepted working name is Inklings.

Inklings represent exploration, game-board activity, seasonal progress, mascot interactions,
mini-games, story progress, challenges, and playful site participation. They are separate from
permanent Nexus Points and the broader spendable-perk role of Embers. Like Embers, they may use a
larger visible number scale when earning and redemption values remain coherent.

================================================================================
2. POSSIBLE SOURCES AND USES
================================================================================

Possible sources:
- daily check-ins and Resting Leaf interactions;
- mascot clicks and verified Easter-egg discovery;
- Relaxation Room visits;
- Campus quizzes and polls;
- event QR scans and campaign participation;
- mini-game and challenge activity;
- eligible booking or purchase activity;
- eligible rating and review actions under the Reward System.

Possible uses:
- game-board movement;
- seasonal missions and challenge boards;
- mascot events;
- Resting Leaf check-ins;
- hidden-site Easter eggs;
- Story Tiles and hidden comic progress;
- mini-game and event progress;
- special unlocks in a game-oriented user panel.

Logging in, clicking repeatedly, or replaying an already completed interaction must not become an
unlimited farming route. Each source needs a versioned eligibility rule, idempotency scope, period
limit, and abuse control under the Reward System.

================================================================================
3. MASCOTS, EASTER EGGS, AND MINI-GAMES
================================================================================

Possible experiences include:
- a hidden mascot on selected pages;
- mascot reactions and find-the-mascot challenges;
- weekly or seasonal hidden-mascot events;
- a badge after an approved discovery sequence;
- hidden comic panels or Story Tiles;
- mini-games connected to The Resting Leaf;
- later native mobile game features.

Rewarded discovery must be based on a server-verifiable event and must respect accessibility,
reduced-motion, keyboard, touch, localization, privacy, and abuse-prevention requirements. Essential
site features and important information must not require a game interaction.

Flash and Java applets are not accepted directions. Web games should use modern maintained
approaches such as HTML5, JavaScript or TypeScript, Canvas, WebGL, Phaser, PixiJS, or carefully
justified Unity WebGL. Native mobile features may use the approved mobile stack.

================================================================================
4. ACCOUNTING AND OPEN DECISIONS
================================================================================

Every Inkling earn, spend, expiry, correction, or reversal is an immutable user_reward event under
the Reward System. user_main may cache the current Inkling balance for display only.

Open decisions:
- final earning and spending rules;
- final seasonal reset or expiry behavior;
- mascot identity and interaction rules;
- Story Tiles and hidden-comic design;
- The Resting Leaf game design;
- game-board, user-panel, and mobile direction;
- final game catalogue, accessibility testing, and anti-farming limits.
