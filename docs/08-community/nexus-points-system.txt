================================================================================
1. MEANING
================================================================================

Nexus Points are the permanent lifetime value of useful approved activity. They are non-spendable
and do not decrease when a user redeems Embers, Inklings, cosmetics, feature credits, or paid-plan
benefits.

Nexus Points are not:
- raw contribution or participation counts;
- a cash balance or transferable asset;
- Reputation or proof of trust;
- a badge, Rank, professional credential, verification, or role;
- moderation, editorial, administrative, establishment, or practitioner authority;
- a benefit that can be purchased directly.

"Permanent" means an earned valid award is not consumed. Fraud, duplication, invalidation, or an
incorrect award may still create an audited correction or reversal in user_reward.

================================================================================
2. POSSIBLE SOURCES
================================================================================

Versioned reward rules may award Nexus Points for:
- approved establishment or practitioner submissions;
- approved profile corrections and data improvements;
- approved articles, reviews, media, or translations;
- useful comments and discussion participation;
- eligible content, spa, therapist, or product ratings;
- eligible completed-experience ratings;
- confirmed valid reports;
- eligible Campus quizzes, surveys, or Community Pulse answers;
- verified event, campaign, or outreach participation;
- eligible bookings or purchases.

An action being listed as a possible source does not guarantee an award. The Reward System owns
shared eligibility, exact schedules, caps, anti-abuse, idempotency, and reversal rules. The source
domain owns whether the underlying activity is approved and valid.

The accepted routine Community Pulse award is one Nexus Point for the first eligible answer to an
exact run question, subject to the Reward System's first-three-per-UTC-day limit. Editing the answer
does not award another point. Full-survey completion may use a separate versioned burden-based rule
without double-awarding questions already rewarded through Community Pulse.

================================================================================
3. DISPLAY AND PROGRESSION
================================================================================

The public profile may show the user's lifetime Nexus Point total when their profile visibility
permits it. user_main may cache this rebuildable total for display; user_reward remains authoritative.

Rank Paths may use eligible Nexus Points as one progression input, together with path-specific
activity and quality requirements. A large point total alone does not grant Rank, Reputation,
badges, verification, authority, or organic visibility.

================================================================================
4. OPEN DECISIONS
================================================================================

- exact Nexus Point awards outside the accepted rating schedule;
- whether any source families need lifetime or rolling caps;
- final public presentation and explanatory text;
- point corrections and appeal thresholds requiring human review.
