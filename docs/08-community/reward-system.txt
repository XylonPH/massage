================================================================================
1. RESPONSIBILITY AND BOUNDARIES
================================================================================

This document owns rules shared by more than one reward unit: eligibility, award schedules,
limits, idempotency, reversals, and the user_reward event model. The focused currency documents
own the meaning, earning boundary, and uses of each unit.

Massage Nexus keeps the following concepts separate:

Concept            | Meaning
-------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Contribution Count | Rebuildable factual count of qualifying user activity.
Nexus Points       | Permanent, non-spendable lifetime value of approved activity.
Embers             | Spendable platform reward currency.
Inklings           | Exploration, seasonal, mascot, and game-progress currency.
Badges             | Visible achievements and recognition.
Rank Paths         | Fictional platform progression titles.
Reputation         | Community-tested trust quality that may rise or fall.
Roles              | Authorization from effective user_access, never from rewards.
User Statistics    | Visible or internal factual activity and participation totals.

A reward never grants workspace authority, professional credentials, verification, moderation
power, rating weight, or Reputation by itself. Payment and reward spending do not purchase those
trust or authority outcomes.

================================================================================
2. SHARED ELIGIBILITY
================================================================================

An activity may produce a reward only when its governing feature marks it eligible and the reward
rule is active for that activity, source, user, and time. Publication, booking completion, rating
validity, contribution approval, or another domain fact remains authoritative in that domain's
record; user_reward records the resulting reward event.

Shared exclusions include rejected, fraudulent, copied, duplicated, self-dealing, connected,
revoked, invalidated, quarantined, replayed, or reward-farming activity unless a focused
specification defines a narrow exception. A scoring weight does not imply reward eligibility, and
receiving a reward does not increase the source item's rating weight or the user's Reputation.

Reward rules must define:
- eligible source and lifecycle state;
- reward unit and amount or versioned formula;
- user, target, period, and daily limits;
- idempotency scope;
- reversal or correction behavior;
- abuse and conflict-of-interest exclusions;
- whether the award is immediate, provisional, or delayed until approval;
- whether later source changes can create a bounded supplemental event.

Minor edits, repeated submissions, source imports, retries, and revoke-then-resubmit flows must not
replay an already granted reward. Manual personal-history records earn no reward by default.

================================================================================
3. RATING REWARDS
================================================================================

The Rating System owns target eligibility, rating modes, experience verification, Xylon Calibrated
Experience Score behavior, connection rules, and the 83-day spa and therapist cycle. This document
owns the reward amounts and reward-ledger limits.

Content reaction reward:

Action                                      | Nexus Points | Embers | Inklings
--------------------------------------------+--------------+--------+---------
Thumbs up/down on an eligible content item  | 1            | 10     | 5

Eligible content may include an article, review, news item, guide, comic, comment, discussion,
media item, or translation. Only the first 25 otherwise eligible thumbs-up/down actions by one user
per UTC reward-ledger day earn rewards. The user may continue reacting after the cap without an
additional reward.

Experience and product rating rewards:

Action                                  | Nexus Points | Embers | Inklings
----------------------------------------+--------------+--------+---------
Eligible simple spa/therapist/product   | 5            | 50     | 20
Eligible criteria-based rating          | 10           | 100    | 40

A criteria-based reward requires at least three eligible target criteria. Honest Not Observed and
Not Applicable answers do not reduce the reward merely because the criterion could not be judged.

One verified-experience bonus may stack with the applicable base reward:

Source or condition                                | Nexus Points | Embers | Inklings
---------------------------------------------------+--------------+--------+---------
Massage Nexus verified completed experience        | +2           | +20    | +10
Confirmed external or on-site experience           | +1           | +10    | +5
User-entered personal history                       | 0            | 0      | 0
Self or connected rating                            | 0            | 0      | 0
Revoked, invalidated, quarantined, or duplicate     | 0            | 0      | 0

The base reward and experience bonus are independent user_reward events so a source upgrade,
duplicate import, edit, reversal, or retry cannot replay either part.

Same-target session limits:
- same user + same spa + same UTC reward-ledger day: one rewarded spa session rating;
- same user + same therapist + same UTC reward-ledger day: one rewarded therapist session rating.

Spa and therapist rating eligibility may refresh after a new valid experience in a new 83-day
cycle. Product-rating rewards do not recur by default unless an approved product-version rule later
defines a genuinely new target version.

================================================================================
4. REVIEW, ARTICLE, MEDIA, AND TRANSLATION REWARDS
================================================================================

Reviews earn more than simple ratings because they require more effort, observation, writing, and
usefulness. The Review Writing Guide owns review publication, quality factors, AI assistance,
multi-author credit, revisions, and abuse handling.

Provisional review maximums:

Review type                    | Nexus Points | Embers             | Inklings
-------------------------------+--------------+--------------------+-------------------
User establishment review      | 500          | To be recalibrated | To be recalibrated
User practitioner review       | 500          | To be recalibrated | To be recalibrated
User product review            | 300          | To be recalibrated | To be recalibrated
Editorial review               | 1,500 total  | To be decided      | To be decided

These are ceilings, not automatic grants. Ember and Inkling values remain unresolved and should
use a gratifying visible scale consistent with their redemption costs. Multi-author rewards are
shared among credited authors rather than duplicated in full. Main review rewards are normally
granted after accepted publication; a later major update can earn additional credit only under an
approved new-experience rule.

Article, media, and translation rewards remain separate from review rewards. Their focused domain
documents own qualifying work and quality factors. Approved publication, originality, usefulness,
research or source quality, human-written effort, completeness, accuracy, readability, media value,
revision quality, and editor confirmation may inform a versioned reward formula. Reading duration
does not itself earn a reward. AI-generated or AI-cleaned portions do not receive the same
user-writing credit as fully user-written work unless a later rule explicitly allows it.

Exact article, media, translation, booking, purchase, and contribution amounts remain unresolved.

================================================================================
5. BOOKINGS, PURCHASES, EVENTS, AND CAMPAIGNS
================================================================================

Eligible bookings, completed experiences, purchases, events, campaigns, polls, quizzes, QR scans,
and other site activity may emit rewards, badge progress, participation statistics, or game
progress. Booking and purchasing do not automatically become contribution counts. Provider-specific
loyalty remains separate from Massage Nexus rewards.

The source domain must emit or expose a stable qualifying fact. Booking, payment, and campaign code
must not directly manipulate balances. Cancelled, no-show, fake, duplicated, refunded where the
rule requires reversal, or unverified manual-history activity receives no normal positive reward.

Social-media actions receive serious rewards only when Massage Nexus can verify the action through
a supported connected identity, platform API, official campaign link, QR event, or comparable
platform-confirmed mechanism. A screenshot alone is not sufficient evidence for a material reward.

================================================================================
6. AUTHORITATIVE DATA AND RECONCILIATION
================================================================================

user_reward stores one immutable earn, spend, expiry, correction, reversal, adjustment, or migration
event for Nexus Points, Embers, or Inklings. Each event identifies the user, reward unit, signed
amount, resulting balance when applicable, source collection and record, reason, rule or formula
version, unique idempotency key, lifecycle status, occurrence time, optional expiry, and reversal
relationship.

Corrections append reversing or replacement events; they do not rewrite settled history. Reward
events and source records must be retained long enough for reconciliation, disputes, fraud review,
and required financial or policy obligations.

user_main caches lifetime Nexus Points and current Ember and Inkling balances with their calculation
time for ordinary profile, header, and workspace display. The projection is rebuildable and is
never sufficient by itself for reconciliation, dispute resolution, expiry, or reversal.
