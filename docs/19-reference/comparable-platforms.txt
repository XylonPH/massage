5. COMPARISON USE
================================================================================

Review comparable platforms for:
- discovery and search;
- profile structure;
- service and price presentation;
- provider verification;
- reviews and ratings;
- booking flow;
- owner and therapist tools;
- promotions and vouchers;
- location and map behavior;
- community interaction;
- content safety and moderation;
- privacy and exploitation risks.

Do not copy another platform's taxonomy, wording, interface, data, or unsafe business model.
