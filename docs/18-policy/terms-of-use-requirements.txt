DOCUMENT PURPOSE
----------------
This document defines what the future public Massage Nexus Terms of Use must cover. It is
not yet the final contract presented to users.

Before public registration, the final Terms must be:
- completed with the legal operator's exact name and contact details;
- checked against the final business model and payment flows;
- checked against Philippine law and applicable target-market law;
- reviewed together with the Privacy Notice and all site-specific policies;
- versioned;
- published in supported languages according to policy;
- accepted through a recorded electronic action.

================================================================================
1. DOCUMENT RELATIONSHIP
================================================================================

The public Terms should work with:
- Privacy Notice;
- Community Guidelines;
- Account and Username Policy;
- NSFW, Mature, and Restricted Content Policy;
- Review and Contribution Guidelines;
- Media Upload Policy;
- Booking, Cancellation, Refund, and Payment policies;
- Provider terms;
- Seller and commerce terms;
- Advertising and sponsorship terms;
- Copyright and intellectual-property process;
- Security and responsible-disclosure information;
- any service-specific consent or eligibility document.

A policy incorporated by reference must be easy to find, versioned, and not silently changed
to create unexpected obligations.

================================================================================
2. ACCEPTANCE AND VERSIONING
================================================================================

Registration for a Massage Nexus membership requires affirmative acceptance of the current
Terms of Use.

Terms acceptance must be separate from:
- acknowledgment of the Privacy Notice;
- marketing email consent;
- promotional SMS consent;
- optional research consent;
- optional AI-processing consent where separate consent is required.

The acceptance record should include:
- account identifier;
- site identifier;
- document type;
- document version;
- locale shown;
- acceptance date and time;
- evidence metadata reasonably required for accountability.

When Terms materially change:
- publish a clear summary;
- identify the effective date;
- require renewed acceptance when appropriate;
- do not rely only on a hidden footer update;
- retain prior versions for legal and audit purposes.

================================================================================
3. ELIGIBILITY
================================================================================

The public Terms must state:
- ordinary public content may be viewed without registration;
- Massage Nexus membership is limited to persons at least 18 years old;
- users must provide accurate eligibility information;
- a person may not create an account for another person without authority;
- a person whose membership was terminated may not evade enforcement through another
  account;
- professional, provider, advertiser, or seller functions may have additional eligibility
  and verification requirements.

Massage Nexus's 18+ membership rule is site-specific and does not automatically describe
every future network site.

================================================================================
4. NETWORK ACCOUNT AND SITE MEMBERSHIP
================================================================================

The Terms should explain:
- one shared account may eventually be used across participating network sites;
- joining Massage Nexus activates only a Massage Nexus membership;
- universal settings may be reused to reduce repeated setup;
- each site may have separate eligibility, terms, privacy information, roles, restrictions,
  and public profiles;
- membership on one site does not automatically create a public profile on another;
- enforcement may be site-specific or network-wide depending on the conduct and documented
  policy.

The final Terms must identify the legal controller or contracting party for the network
account and for Massage Nexus membership.

================================================================================
5. ACCOUNT RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

Users must:
- provide accurate required information;
- protect credentials and recovery methods;
- use only accounts and identities they are authorized to use;
- notify Massage Nexus of suspected unauthorized access;
- keep verified contact methods current where reasonably possible;
- not share credentials for deceptive, abusive, or prohibited purposes;
- not impersonate platform staff, providers, other users, or organizations;
- comply with username and protected-name rules;
- comply with security checks and reauthentication requirements.

Massage Nexus may:
- reject a username;
- require verification;
- lock an account after suspicious activity;
- revoke sessions;
- require a password reset;
- restrict, suspend, or terminate a membership;
- preserve a deleted or banned username to prevent impersonation.

================================================================================
6. PUBLIC DIRECTORY AND PROFILE INFORMATION
================================================================================

The Terms must explain that Massage Nexus may contain:
- claimed profiles;
- verified profiles;
- unclaimed profiles;
- community-contributed information;
- historical, closed, moved, disputed, or incomplete records;
- information from providers, users, public sources, or approved imports.

A listing does not automatically mean:
- the provider endorsed Massage Nexus;
- Massage Nexus verified every fact;
- the provider is currently operating;
- availability is live;
- a booking is confirmed;
- a service is legal in every location;
- a provider is licensed for every claimed activity.

Material status labels and source information should be displayed where appropriate.

Users and providers should have processes to:
- suggest corrections;
- claim eligible profiles;
- report impersonation;
- dispute inaccurate material;
- provide evidence;
- appeal applicable moderation or verification decisions.

================================================================================
7. USER CONTENT AND CONTRIBUTIONS
================================================================================

User content may include:
- reviews;
- ratings;
- comments;
- discussions;
- corrections;
- profile information;
- photographs;
- videos;
- animated media;
- translations;
- sources;
- booking-related notes;
- other approved submissions.

Users retain ownership of content they own.

The Terms should request only the license reasonably needed to:
- host;
- store;
- reproduce;
- display;
- format;
- resize;
- transcode;
- translate;
- distribute within approved Massage Nexus and network surfaces;
- moderate;
- protect;
- promote the relevant feature;
- preserve evidence and history where justified.

The final license must:
- be understandable;
- not claim ownership of user content;
- address public and private content separately;
- address deletion and retained copies;
- address translated and transformed media;
- address backups and legal holds;
- address content contributed to shared directory records.

Users must have rights and permission to submit their content and depictions of other people.

================================================================================
8. REVIEWS, RATINGS, AND COMMUNITY CONDUCT
================================================================================

The Terms and related guidelines must prohibit:
- fabricated experiences;
- undisclosed conflicts of interest;
- review manipulation;
- paid positive reviews without approved disclosure;
- threats or retaliation;
- harassment;
- doxxing;
- private health or contact disclosure;
- impersonation;
- spam;
- illegal solicitation;
- exploitation;
- sexual harassment;
- discriminatory abuse;
- misleading edited media;
- coordinated abuse;
- attempts to bypass moderation.

The platform may preserve criticism and negative experiences when they are relevant and
lawful. Removal must not be promised merely because a provider dislikes a review.

The platform may:
- label;
- reduce distribution;
- request evidence;
- restrict replies;
- lock discussion;
- remove;
- preserve for investigation;
- suspend;
- refer serious matters to appropriate authorities when legally required.

================================================================================
9. NSFW, MATURE, AND RESTRICTED CONTENT
================================================================================

The Terms must incorporate the separate NSFW, Mature, and Restricted Content Policy.

Key points:
- ordinary public content remains safe for broad viewing;
- mature or NSFW content is hidden by default;
- restricted access requires an active verified adult Massage Nexus membership and explicit
  user choice where policy permits;
- self-declared birth date is not necessarily strong age verification;
- some content may require stronger assurance, geographic restriction, or complete
  prohibition;
- avatars, cover images, ordinary thumbnails, advertising creatives, and incidental public
  surfaces must not contain NSFW material;
- illegal, exploitative, non-consensual, or minor-related sexual content is prohibited;
- a content label does not make otherwise prohibited content acceptable.

================================================================================
10. BOOKING AND PROVIDER RELATIONSHIPS
================================================================================

The final Terms must distinguish:
- Massage Nexus as a discovery and technology platform;
- independent establishments, therapists, sellers, and other providers;
- direct provider terms and policies;
- bookings confirmed through Massage Nexus;
- inquiries or plans for unclaimed providers;
- external booking links;
- walk-in records;
- provider-created records.

Unless expressly stated otherwise, providers are independent and responsible for:
- services;
- qualifications;
- licenses;
- professional conduct;
- facilities;
- staff;
- prices;
- taxes;
- availability;
- provider policies;
- service safety;
- legal compliance.

Massage Nexus must not represent an unclaimed provider inquiry as a confirmed booking.

Booking terms should cover:
- participant responsibility;
- accurate intake;
- eligibility;
- consent;
- cancellation;
- no-show;
- lateness;
- deposits;
- refunds;
- payment disputes;
- provider cancellation;
- service changes;
- emergency and safety behavior;
- provider-specific terms.

================================================================================
11. HEALTH, WELLNESS, AND SAFETY DISCLAIMER
================================================================================

Massage Nexus provides directory, educational, community, and platform information.

The Terms should explain:
- content is not a diagnosis;
- content is not individualized medical advice;
- AI-generated or translated content may require human verification;
- users should consult qualified professionals for medical concerns;
- emergency situations require appropriate emergency services;
- providers remain responsible for their professional scope and service decisions;
- users must disclose relevant information through approved intake when booking;
- no platform content guarantees a particular health result.

This disclaimer must not be used to excuse negligent platform conduct or remove rights that
cannot legally be waived.

================================================================================
12. PAYMENTS, COMMERCE, AND FINANCIAL RECORDS
================================================================================

Before accepting money, the Terms must identify:
- who receives the payment;
- whether Massage Nexus acts as merchant, agent, marketplace, or technology provider;
- applicable fees;
- taxes;
- deposit handling;
- refunds;
- chargebacks;
- payout timing;
- payment-provider involvement;
- vouchers and gift cards;
- abandoned or dormant balances if ever applicable;
- currency and exchange issues;
- payment-document responsibility.

Massage Nexus does not initially operate a general stored-value wallet. A future wallet
requires a separate financial, legal, fraud, security, reconciliation, and regulatory
specification.

The site must not store full payment-card details unless an approved, compliant architecture
requires it. Prefer approved payment-provider tokens.

================================================================================
13. EXTERNAL LINKS AND THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
================================================================================

The Terms should explain that third parties may provide:
- maps;
- social login;
- email;
- SMS;
- payment processing;
- storage;
- analytics;
- AI;
- translation;
- external booking;
- provider websites;
- embedded content.

Third-party terms and privacy practices may apply.

Massage Nexus should not disclaim all responsibility for vendor selection, configuration, or
data handling. It remains responsible for obligations that apply to it.

================================================================================
14. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
================================================================================

The Terms should cover:
- Massage Nexus names, logos, visual identity, software, text, taxonomy, and original media;
- third-party marks and content;
- user-owned content;
- permitted personal use;
- prohibited scraping, copying, reselling, or misleading reuse;
- API and data-license rules;
- copyright complaint and counter-notice process where applicable;
- repeat infringement response;
- preservation of lawful quotation, review, commentary, and other applicable rights.

Do not claim ownership over factual information merely because it appears in a database.
Rights in selection, structure, original descriptions, media, software, and branding should
be described separately.

================================================================================
15. PROHIBITED USE
================================================================================

Prohibited conduct should include:
- unauthorized access;
- credential attacks;
- malware;
- scraping that violates approved access rules;
- denial-of-service activity;
- bypassing rate limits;
- reverse engineering where lawfully restrictable and not conflicting with mandatory rights;
- data harvesting;
- doxxing;
- stalking;
- fraud;
- impersonation;
- exploitation;
- trafficking;
- illegal solicitation;
- harassment;
- extortion;
- false claims;
- spam;
- review manipulation;
- upload of prohibited content;
- evasion of bans;
- interference with provider or user accounts;
- misuse of private booking, health, contact, or location information;
- disclosure of confidential security information for harmful purposes.

Responsible security research should be handled through a separate disclosure process and
must not be confused with malicious exploitation.

================================================================================
16. MODERATION, RESTRICTION, SUSPENSION, AND APPEAL
================================================================================

The Terms should reserve proportionate actions:
- warning;
- content label;
- visibility limit;
- feature restriction;
- temporary lock;
- content removal;
- site-membership suspension;
- network-account security lock;
- termination;
- evidence preservation;
- referral where required.

Where safe and appropriate, users should receive:
- reason category;
- duration;
- affected content or feature;
- appeal route;
- required correction;
- final result.

Immediate action may occur for urgent safety, fraud, legal, or security risk.

================================================================================
17. ACCOUNT CLOSURE, DELETION, AND RETENTION
================================================================================

The Terms should explain:
- how a user requests closure;
- any deletion grace period;
- how the request can be canceled;
- what becomes unavailable immediately;
- what may be retained for security, fraud, tax, booking, dispute, consent, legal, backup,
  and legitimate record purposes;
- how public contributions are handled;
- whether content is deleted, anonymized, detached, or retained;
- how managed business records differ from the personal account;
- how a site membership differs from the shared network account.

Exact retention belongs in the Privacy Notice and retention schedule.

================================================================================
18. SERVICE CHANGES AND AVAILABILITY
================================================================================

The Terms should allow reasonable changes to:
- features;
- supported countries;
- providers;
- interfaces;
- storage;
- account methods;
- policies;
- pricing;
- integrations.

Users should receive meaningful notice when changes materially affect rights, payments,
privacy, or account use.

Do not promise uninterrupted or error-free availability.

Maintenance, security events, external-service failures, force majeure, and development
changes may affect service, but these clauses must not remove non-waivable consumer rights.

================================================================================
19. DISCLAIMERS, LIABILITY, INDEMNITY, AND DISPUTES
================================================================================

These provisions require qualified legal review before publication.

The final Terms may address:
- directory accuracy;
- provider conduct;
- service results;
- user content;
- external services;
- service availability;
- damages;
- exclusions not permitted by law;
- liability caps where enforceable;
- user responsibility for unlawful submissions;
- dispute escalation;
- governing law;
- venue;
- mediation or arbitration only if appropriate and lawful;
- consumer rights that remain unaffected.

Do not copy aggressive clauses from another platform without reviewing whether they are
lawful, proportionate, understandable, and suitable for Massage Nexus.

================================================================================
20. CONTACT AND NOTICES
================================================================================

The public Terms should identify:
- legal operator;
- business address where required;
- general support contact;
- legal contact;
- privacy contact or DPO;
- copyright contact;
- security disclosure contact;
- how formal notices are delivered.

Security, privacy, legal, and ordinary support channels should not all be one unmanaged
mailbox.
