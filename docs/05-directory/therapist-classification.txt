Title: Massage Nexus Therapist Classification
Document Version: 1.40
Date Created: 2026-07-11
Revision Date: 2026-07-20
Document Description: Defines the controlled fields and option sets used to classify massage therapists and their professional service contexts in Massage Nexus, including recommended snake_case field names, stable option codes, labels, and internal descriptions.

This is a compact taxonomy reference, not public documentation, a database schema, a therapist-profile specification, a credential schema, or a scheduling system. Some fields apply to the therapist generally; Work Arrangement, Provider Rank, Therapist Service Market Class, Client Access, and Booking Requirement may instead apply to a specific establishment affiliation or independent-practice context.

GENERAL RULES
-------------
- Use Therapist as the public Massage Nexus term.
- Do not create one broad Therapist Type field that mixes profession, work arrangement, specialty, service, technique, and training status.
- Work Arrangement and Provider Rank describe a particular professional context and may differ across affiliations.
- Availability Status describes acceptance or provision of service in an applicable professional
  context. Professional Practice Status describes the therapist's overall current practice.
- Person life status and record lifecycle remain separate from both availability and professional
  practice status.
- Provider Rank records a rank assigned by an establishment or organized provider; it does not prove credentials or quality.
- Therapist Service Market Class describes the market position of a specific therapist service context, not the permanent worth or skill of the person.
- Exact provider-specific rank titles may be preserved separately from the normalized Provider Rank.
- Massage systems, named services, techniques, and capabilities are represented through service relationships rather than Specialty Focus.
- Unconfirmed classifications remain unselected and may be explained through a separate internal note.

================================================================================
1. WORK ARRANGEMENT
================================================================================

Field Name: type_work_arrangement
Field Label: Work Arrangement
Selection: Single-select per professional context

Code | Label       | Description
-----|-------------|------------------------------------------------------------
EMP  | Employee    | Works as an employee or regular staff member of an organization.
IND  | Independent | Operates independently and controls their own professional practice.
CTR  | Contractor  | Provides services through a contract, assignment, agency, or service agreement.

Trainee is not a Work Arrangement. Training status belongs to education, credential, learner, or Provider Rank records.

================================================================================
2. PRACTICE SETTING
================================================================================

Field Name: type_practice_setting
Field Label: Practice Setting
Selection: Multi-select

Code | Label                     | Description
-----|---------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
SP   | Spa                       | Massage spa, day spa, salon spa, hotel spa, or comparable spa setting.
SL   | Salon                     | Beauty, grooming, nail, or salon establishment offering massage.
WC   | Wellness Center           | Broader wellness establishment combining massage with other disciplines.
PS   | Private Studio            | Dedicated professional treatment room or independent practice space.
CL   | Clinic                    | Clinic or health-oriented practice offering massage or bodywork.
RF   | Rehabilitation Facility   | Facility focused on rehabilitation, recovery, or restorative care.
HP   | Hospital                  | Hospital or hospital-operated service environment.
HT   | Hotel                     | Hotel treatment area, hotel spa, or hotel-operated guest service.
RS   | Resort                    | Resort spa, destination property, or resort-operated service.
BF   | Bathing Facility          | Bathhouse, hot-spring facility, thermal bath, hammam, or similar setting.
FF   | Fitness Facility          | Gym, fitness center, or health club.
SF   | Sports Facility           | Athletic center, sports club, stadium facility, or training venue.
CS   | Corporate Site            | Workplace, office, company campus, or employer-sponsored setting.
EV   | Event Site                | Convention, expo, fair, private event, pop-up, or organized temporary event.
CR   | Client Residence          | Client's house, apartment, condominium, or other residence.
TR   | Therapist Residence       | Therapist's own residence where clients are received.
TC   | Training Center           | Massage school, academy, vocational institution, or supervised training clinic.
CF   | Community Facility        | Community center, civic facility, nonprofit venue, or public-service location.
RC   | Residential Care Facility | Senior residence, nursing facility, assisted-living site, or long-term care facility.
RT   | Retreat Center            | Wellness, meditation, recovery, or residential retreat setting.
TH   | Transportation Hub        | Airport, railway station, bus terminal, or similar passenger facility.
MF   | Maritime Facility         | Cruise ship, ferry, passenger vessel, yacht, port facility, or maritime workplace.
MU   | Mobile Treatment Unit     | Vehicle, trailer, or transportable purpose-built treatment room.
OV   | Outdoor Venue             | Beach, park, campsite, outdoor resort area, or arranged outdoor treatment site.

Practice Setting identifies recurring professional environments, not every place where massage could theoretically occur.

================================================================================
3. PROVIDER RANK
================================================================================

Field Name: position_affiliation
Field Label: Provider Rank
Selection: Single-select per establishment or organized-provider affiliation

Code | Label           | Description
-----|-----------------|------------------------------------------------------------
TRN  | Trainee         | Provides service under training, practice, or supervised competency development.
JNR  | Junior          | Entry-level provider rank formally used by the establishment.
REG  | Regular         | Normal established provider rank without a senior or specialist designation.
SEN  | Senior          | Higher internal rank based on experience, responsibility, demand, or provider policy.
SPC  | Specialist      | Provider-designated rank for a recognized specialist service area.
MST  | Master          | Highest or advanced provider-designated rank within the establishment's own system.
VEX  | Visiting Expert | Temporarily scheduled expert, trainer, master, or specialist from another location or organization.

Rules
-----
- The establishment or authorized provider may submit its own rank.
- The exact provider title, such as Master Reflexologist, should be preserved separately.
- The therapist may confirm or dispute the affiliation and rank.
- Master, Specialist, or Visiting Expert does not automatically prove licensure, credential verification, or superior review results.
- Independent therapists normally have no Provider Rank unless an organized provider has formally assigned one.

================================================================================
4. THERAPIST SERVICE MARKET CLASS
================================================================================

Field Name: level_therapist_service_market
Field Label: Therapist Service Market Class
Selection: Single-select per establishment or independent-practice service context

Code | Label    | Description
-----|----------|--------------------------------------------------------------
S    | Elite    | Exceptional premium service context with very high price, personalization, presentation, demand, or specialist positioning.
A    | Premium  | Clearly upscale professional service context above ordinary market standards.
B    | Standard | Mainstream professional service with normal expectations for reliability, cleanliness, safety, and competence.
C    | Budget   | Affordable professional service with simpler presentation, equipment, or commercial structure.
D    | Basic    | Entry-level, trainee, short, express, or minimal service context.

Philippine Planning Reference
-----------------------------
Use a directly attributable 60-minute therapist service rate when available:
- S: usually PHP 2,500 or more;
- A: usually PHP 1,500 to 2,499;
- B: usually PHP 700 to 1,499;
- C: usually PHP 350 to 699;
- D: usually below PHP 350 or primarily short basic services.

Rules
-----
- The field applies to a service context, not permanently to the therapist profile.
- A therapist may have different market classes across a spa, hotel, event, or independent practice.
- The establishment or therapist may provide evidence, but Massage Nexus assigns or confirms the class.
- Price alone does not determine class.
- Credentials, Provider Rank, ratings, reviews, experience, and market class remain separate.
- Other countries and regions require locally reviewed price references rather than direct currency conversion.

================================================================================
5. SPECIALTY FOCUS
================================================================================

Field Name: type_specialty_focus
Field Label: Specialty Focus
Selection: Multi-select

Code | Label                      | Description
-----|----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
RLX  | Relaxation                 | Focuses on calm, comfort, reduced tension, and restful service.
MDS  | Muscle Discomfort Support  | Focuses on muscle tension and discomfort within permitted massage scope.
DP   | Deep Pressure              | Focuses on clients preferring sustained, firm, or deeper controlled pressure.
SR   | Sports Recovery            | Focuses on exercise fatigue, athletic recovery, and performance support.
MB   | Mobility                   | Focuses on movement comfort, range of motion, and mobility-oriented bodywork.
ST   | Stretching                 | Uses assisted or guided stretching as a meaningful service focus.
STR  | Stress Relief              | Focuses on physical and mental tension associated with stress or daily strain.
SL   | Sleep Support              | Focuses on relaxation-oriented services intended to support rest and sleep readiness.
SC   | Senior Care                | Adapts pressure, pace, positioning, communication, and accessibility for older clients.
PN   | Perinatal Support          | Focuses on appropriately trained pregnancy and postnatal service.
RC   | Recovery Support           | Supports fatigue, exertion, inactivity, travel, or non-acute physical strain.
WF   | Workplace Fatigue          | Focuses on occupational strain, desk work, standing, repetition, and commuting fatigue.
GC   | Gentle Care                | Focuses on light pressure, slower pacing, cautious handling, and comfort adaptation.

Hilot, Thai Massage, Shiatsu, Swedish Massage, Deep Tissue Massage, Oncology Massage, Manual Lymphatic Drainage, Reflexology, Hot Stone Massage, and similar named services belong under service capability rather than Specialty Focus.

================================================================================
6. AVAILABILITY STATUS
================================================================================

Field Name: status_therapist_availability
Field Label: Availability Status
Selection: Single-select per applicable context

Code | Label                  | Description
-----|------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
AV   | Available              | Actively accepting or providing services under normal conditions.
LM   | Limited Availability   | Accepting services only within restricted days, hours, locations, services, or capacity.
EC   | Existing Clients Only  | Continuing current client relationships but not accepting new clients.
TU   | Temporarily Unavailable| Not presently accepting or providing services but expected to return.
UN   | Unknown                | Current availability cannot be confirmed.

Inactive and Retired describe overall professional practice rather than availability in one
service context. Death is a separate person life-status fact. None of these belong in Availability
Status.

================================================================================
7. PROFESSIONAL PRACTICE STATUS
================================================================================

Field Name: status_therapist_practice
Field Label: Professional Practice Status
Selection: Single-select per therapist

Code | Label    | Description
-----|----------|------------------------------------------------------------
AC   | Active   | Has confirmed current professional practice in at least one context.
IN   | Inactive | Has no confirmed current professional service activity but has not confirmed retirement.
RT   | Retired  | Has ended professional practice.
UN   | Unknown  | Current professional practice cannot be confirmed.

Rules
-----
- Stale availability, an empty schedule, temporary unavailability, or one ended affiliation does
  not by itself prove Inactive or Retired.
- Retired may preserve a historical public profile and may later return to Active after appropriate
  confirmation.
- Professional Practice Status is separate from person life status, profile claim, moderation,
  verification, and record lifecycle.

================================================================================
8. BOOKING REQUIREMENT
================================================================================

Field Name: mode_therapist_booking
Field Label: Booking Requirement
Selection: Single-select per applicable context

Code | Label                 | Description
-----|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
WI   | Walk-In Accepted      | May accept clients without an advance booking.
AP   | Appointment Preferred | Advance booking is encouraged, but unscheduled service may be accepted.
AR   | Appointment Required  | Service must be arranged in advance.

On Call is a scheduling arrangement and is not retained in this classification document.

================================================================================
9. CLIENT ACCESS
================================================================================

Field Name: type_client_access
Field Label: Client Access
Selection: Single-select per applicable practice context

Code | Label                | Description
-----|----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
OA   | Open Access          | Normally accepts eligible members of the general public.
MO   | Men Only             | Accepts only male clients for the represented service context.
WO   | Women Only           | Accepts only female clients for the represented service context.
CO   | Couples Only         | Accepts only paired or couple bookings.
MR   | Members Only         | Serves only registered members of an establishment, club, or program.
PG   | Property Guests Only | Serves only authorized guests of a host property or organization.
PC   | Private Clientele    | Accepts only invited, referred, approved, or selectively accepted clients.

A therapist may have different Client Access rules at different affiliations or service contexts.

================================================================================
10. CLIENT FOCUS
================================================================================

Field Name: target_client_focus
Field Label: Client Focus
Selection: Multi-select

Code | Label             | Description
-----|-------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
GP   | General Public    | Serves a broad clientele rather than one specialized group.
MN   | Men               | Has services or experience relevant to male clients.
WM   | Women             | Has services or experience relevant to female clients.
LQ   | LGBTQ Community   | Explicitly welcomes or focuses on LGBTQ clients and related community needs.
CP   | Couples           | Supports couple-oriented sessions, paired services, or couple bookings.
FM   | Families          | Supports appropriate family-oriented service arrangements.
SR   | Seniors           | Has experience adapting service for older clients.
PR   | Pregnant Clients  | Provides appropriately trained and safety-conscious pregnancy service.
PN   | Postnatal Clients | Provides appropriately trained service after childbirth.
AT   | Athletes          | Supports sports recovery, training fatigue, mobility, or athletic needs.
TR   | Tourists          | Is prepared for traveler needs, hotel service, language support, or short-term booking.
OW   | Office Workers    | Focuses on workplace stress, occupational strain, repetition, or commuting fatigue.

Client Focus does not restrict access unless Client Access says otherwise.

================================================================================
11. CREDENTIAL VERIFICATION STATUS
================================================================================

Field Name: status_credential_verification
Field Label: Credential Verification Status
Selection: Single-select per credential record

Code | Label         | Description
-----|---------------|------------------------------------------------------------
SD   | Self-Declared | Credential information is claimed without confirmed supporting evidence.
SB   | Submitted     | Supporting information has been submitted and awaits review.
VF   | Verified      | Credential has been reviewed and verified under Massage Nexus rules.
NR   | Needs Review  | Information is incomplete, conflicting, disputed, or requires additional assessment.
RJ   | Rejected      | Submitted credential claim was not accepted after review.

================================================================================
12. CREDENTIAL LIFECYCLE STATUS
================================================================================

Field Name: status_credential_lifecycle
Field Label: Credential Lifecycle Status
Selection: Single-select per credential record

Code | Label     | Description
-----|-----------|------------------------------------------------------------
AC   | Active    | Credential is currently valid according to available information.
EX   | Expired   | Credential has passed its stated validity period.
SU   | Suspended | Credential has been formally suspended by the issuer or responsible authority.
RV   | Revoked   | Credential has been formally withdrawn or cancelled.
UN   | Unknown   | Current lifecycle condition cannot be confirmed.

A public profile summary should be derived from individual credential records rather than stored as one conflicting profile-level status.
