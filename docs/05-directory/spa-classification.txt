DOCUMENT DESCRIPTION
--------------------
This document defines the controlled fields and option sets used to classify spas and spa-like establishments in Massage Nexus. It includes recommended snake_case field names, stable option codes, concise labels, and internal descriptions.

This is a compact taxonomy reference, not public documentation, a database schema, a profile specification, or an implementation guide. Unconfirmed classifications should remain unselected and may be explained through a separate internal note. Labels may be translated later without changing their stable option codes.

GENERAL RULES
-------------
- Technical field names use English snake_case.
- Option codes use stable uppercase letters, normally one to three characters.
- Spa Type, Spa Market Class, Physical Setting, Access Model, Operation Model, Establishment Status, Treatment Area Type, Privacy Level, Treatment Area Capacity, and Client Access are single-select.
- Service Delivery Mode and Client Focus are multi-select.
- Spa Type identifies the establishment's primary identity; actual services describe additional offerings.
- Market Class provides comparison context and does not determine rating, verification, price, or quality by itself.
- Establishment Status describes real-world operation, not record lifecycle or moderation.
- Website quality, subscription plan, custom domain, Branded Portal, and Massage Campus participation do not affect classification.

================================================================================
1. SPA TYPE
================================================================================

Field Name: type_spa
Field Label: Spa Type
Selection: Single-select

Code | Label                         | Description
-----|-------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
MS   | Massage Spa                   | Establishment primarily centered on massage services.
DY   | Day Spa                       | Non-residential spa offering treatments completed within one visit.
SL   | Salon Spa                     | Salon or grooming business with a meaningful spa and wellness operation.
NL   | Nail Spa                      | Nail-focused establishment also offering hand, foot, or related spa services.
BT   | Beauty Spa                    | Beauty-focused spa offering facial, skin, body, slimming, or related treatments.
RF   | Reflexology Center            | Establishment primarily centered on reflexology and related pressure-based services.
BH   | Bathhouse                     | Communal or facility-based bathing and relaxation establishment.
HY   | Hydrotherapy Spa              | Spa primarily centered on water-based treatment or hydrotherapy equipment.
WC   | Wellness Center               | Broader establishment combining massage with several wellness disciplines or programs.
SP   | Specialty Wellness Facility   | Establishment centered on a distinctive wellness system, format, or technology.
MD   | Medical Spa                   | Spa providing medically supervised or regulated aesthetic and wellness procedures.
HT   | Hotel Spa                     | Spa situated within or directly operated as part of a hotel.
RS   | Resort Spa                    | Spa situated within or directly operated as part of a resort.
DT   | Destination Spa               | Wellness destination that is itself a principal reason for travel or an extended stay.
HP   | Home Service Provider         | Organized provider primarily sending therapists to clients rather than serving a public branch.
MB   | Mobile Spa                    | Portable spa operation designed to serve changing venues with deployable equipment and staff.
TC   | Training Clinic               | Supervised client-service facility connected to massage or wellness training.
HS   | Hot Spring Bathing Facility   | Bathing establishment centered on natural or geothermally heated water.

Classification Notes
--------------------
- A conventional spa that also offers home service keeps its normal Spa Type and uses Home Service under Service Delivery Mode.
- Mobile Spa is reserved for an operation whose identity is genuinely portable, not merely any spa that accepts off-site bookings.
- Regional terms such as Onsen may be retained as aliases or descriptive terminology without replacing the normalized label.

================================================================================
2. SPA MARKET CLASS
================================================================================

Field Name: level_spa_market
Field Label: Spa Market Class
Selection: Single-select

Code | Label    | Description
-----|----------|--------------------------------------------------------------
S    | Luxury   | Elite, highly refined spa experience with substantial premium facilities, privacy, service flow, and presentation.
A    | Premium  | Clearly upscale spa experience above ordinary professional standards.
B    | Standard | Dependable mainstream professional spa with appropriate cleanliness, safety, privacy, and organization.
C    | Budget   | Affordable spa with simpler facilities and presentation but normal expectations for safety, respect, honesty, and value.
D    | Basic    | Minimal or entry-level establishment focused on essential, short, shared, or limited-amenity service.

Philippine Planning Reference
-----------------------------
Use the establishment's typical standard 60-minute individual massage when available:
- S: usually PHP 2,500 or more;
- A: usually PHP 1,500 to 2,499;
- B: usually PHP 700 to 1,499;
- C: usually PHP 350 to 699;
- D: usually below PHP 350 or primarily short basic services.

The ranges are internal Philippine references only. Other markets require locally reviewed thresholds based on normal prices, wages, facilities, and purchasing conditions. Price alone never determines class.

Field Label: Physical Setting
Selection: Single-select

Code | Label                 | Description
-----|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
ST   | Standalone Storefront | Dedicated street-level or freestanding premises.
CU   | Commercial Unit       | Unit inside a commercial building, retail strip, or business complex.
ML   | Mall                  | Premises inside a shopping mall or major retail center.
HT   | Hotel                 | Premises physically located within a hotel property.
RS   | Resort                | Premises physically located within a resort property.
RU   | Residential Unit      | Spa operating from a property organized or classified as residential.
OR   | Owner Residence       | Business receiving clients directly in the owner's or operator's home.
OB   | Office Building       | Premises inside a building primarily used for offices.
CS   | Corporate Site        | Premises inside a company campus, headquarters, or private organizational site.
EV   | Event Site            | Temporary location used specifically for an organized event.
PU   | Pop-Up Site           | Temporary commercial location operating for a limited run.
HC   | Healthcare Facility   | Premises inside a clinic, rehabilitation, medical, or healthcare property.
FT   | Fitness Facility      | Premises inside a gym, sports club, or fitness center.
TR   | Training Center       | Premises inside a school, academy, vocational center, or training facility.

Physical Setting may remain unselected when a Home Service Provider or Mobile Spa has no suitable fixed service premises.

================================================================================
4. SERVICE DELIVERY MODE
================================================================================

Field Name: mode_service_delivery
Field Label: Service Delivery Mode
Selection: Multi-select

Code | Label                     | Description
-----|---------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
OS   | On-Site Service           | Clients receive service at provider-controlled premises.
HM   | Home Service              | Service is delivered at a client's residence.
HR   | Hotel Room Service        | Service is delivered inside guest lodging.
CP   | Corporate Service         | Service is delivered through a workplace or employer arrangement.
EV   | Event Service             | Service is delivered for an organized event or temporary function.
MB   | Mobile Service            | Provider can deploy portable equipment and personnel to changing locations.
PA   | Provider-Arranged Off-Site| Provider delivers service at an off-site location it selects or arranges that does not fit a more specific off-site code.

Mobile Service describes portable capability and may be selected with Home Service, Hotel Room Service, Corporate Service, or Event Service.

Provider-Arranged Off-Site is a catch-all off-site delivery context, not a Spa Type (see docs/05-directory/spa-profile.txt section 17). Use Home Service, Hotel Room Service, Corporate Service, or Event Service instead whenever the booking actually fits one of those more specific codes; reserve Provider-Arranged Off-Site for an off-site arrangement that does not.

5. ACCESS MODEL
================================================================================

Field Name: mode_access
Field Label: Access Model
Selection: Single-select

Code | Label                 | Description
-----|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
WI   | Walk-In               | Clients are normally accepted without prior booking.
AP   | Appointment Preferred | Advance booking is encouraged, but walk-ins may be accepted.
AR   | Appointment Required  | Service must normally be arranged before arrival.
EA   | Event Access          | Service is available only through a particular event or scheduled engagement.
RE   | Restricted Entry      | Entry follows a special rule not fully represented by ordinary booking access.

Membership, property-guest, referral, invitation, and clientele restrictions belong under Client Access rather than Access Model.

6. OPERATION MODEL
================================================================================

Field Name: type_establishment_operation
Field Label: Operation Model
Selection: Single-select

Code | Label                      | Description
-----|----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
IN   | Independent Operation      | Standalone business not operating as a branch, franchise, department, collective, or managed network.
OB   | Owner-Operated Branch      | Branch directly owned and operated by the brand or organization owner.
FR   | Franchise-Operated Branch  | Branch operated by a franchise holder under another brand's system.
HO   | Hotel-Operated             | Spa directly managed as part of a hotel organization.
RO   | Resort-Operated            | Spa directly managed as part of a resort organization.
CL   | Clinic-Operated            | Spa or massage service directly managed by a clinic or healthcare organization.
TC   | Training-Center-Operated   | Client-service operation directly managed by a school or training institution.
CO   | Therapist Collective       | Therapists operating under a shared identity or coordinated service arrangement.
HN   | Home Service Network       | Managed group coordinating several therapists for off-site bookings.
TM   | Temporary Operation        | Seasonal, experimental, event-based, or limited-run operation.
SP   | Shared Practice Facility   | Facility renting rooms, stations, equipment, or shared support to independent providers.

A marketplace relationship, directory listing, or booking partnership does not by itself determine Operation Model.

Field Label: Establishment Status
Selection: Single-select

Code | Label                | Description
-----|----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------

PC   | Permanently Closed   | Permanently stopped operating at the listed location.

8. TREATMENT AREA TYPE
================================================================================

Field Name: type_treatment_area
Field Label: Treatment Area Type
Selection: Single-select per treatment area

Code | Label            | Description
-----|------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
ER   | Enclosed Room    | Space fully enclosed by permanent walls and normally entered through a door.
PA   | Partitioned Area | Station separated by curtains, screens, dividers, or partial walls.
OA   | Open Area        | Station with little structural separation from surrounding activity.
PS   | Portable Setup   | Temporary area created with portable beds, chairs, mats, screens, or equipment.

VIP designation, equipment, wet-treatment capability, and accessibility features are separate properties.

Field Label: Treatment Privacy Level

Code | Label        | Description
-----|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------
PV   | Private      | Strong visual and physical separation for one booking party.
SP   | Semi-Private | Partial separation with possible sound transfer, shared access, or nearby activity.
SH   | Shared       | Several unrelated clients or treatment stations use the same room or area.
OP   | Open         | Little or no meaningful privacy separation.

C    | Couple     | Designed primarily for two clients receiving service together.

================================================================================
11. CLIENT ACCESS
================================================================================

Field Name: type_client_access
Field Label: Client Access
Selection: Single-select

Code | Label                | Description
-----|----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
OA   | Open Access          | Normally accepts eligible members of the general public.
MO   | Men Only             | Access is restricted to male clients.
WO   | Women Only           | Access is restricted to female clients.
CO   | Couples Only         | Access is restricted to paired or couple bookings.
MR   | Members Only         | Access is restricted to registered members.
PG   | Property Guests Only | Access is restricted to authorized guests of a host property or organization.
PC   | Private Clientele    | Access is limited to invited, referred, approved, or selectively accepted clients.

Client Access describes an actual restriction. It must not be inferred from marketing style, therapist gender, LGBTQ focus, nudity policy, or adult-service assumptions.

FM   | Families          | Accommodates family-oriented visits or service arrangements.
SR   | Seniors           | Provides facilities or services designed with older clients in mind.
PR   | Pregnant Clients  | Provides appropriately trained and safety-conscious pregnancy services.
PN   | Postnatal Clients | Provides services intended for clients after childbirth.

Client Focus describes intentional service relevance and does not restrict access unless Client Access says otherwise.

RELATED STRUCTURE TAXONOMY
--------------------------

The additional controlled fields used by directory research structures are grouped by meaning in
data/taxonomy/massage_nexus/directory_place.json, operating_schedule.json,
organization_relationship.json, service_offering.json, practitioner_professional.json,
research_verification.json, and claim_management.json. They cover coordinates and address
visibility, operating exceptions and access periods, organization/person relationships,
provider-menu offerings and prices, practitioner professional facts, evidence review, and claims.

These fields supplement rather than replace the classification fields in this document. Facility,
amenity, treatment-area, and accessibility summaries reuse the existing establishment taxonomy.
Current establishment_main samples use DY, B, CU, OS, AP, IN, OP, OA, and GP; the older stale
DAY/MID/COM/ONS/APO/IND/OPN/PUB/GEN sample spellings are not accepted option codes.
