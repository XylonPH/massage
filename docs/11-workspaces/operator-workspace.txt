The accepted workspace name is Operator Workspace. Operator is the broad functional term for an
authorized person using it; the person may be a legal owner, branch manager, franchise operator,
hotel or resort representative, administrator, receptionist, marketing staff member, finance
staff member, agency representative, or another authorized participant.

The workspace supports official business information, branches, services, prices, therapists, facilities, hours, contact details, media, promotions, bookings, messages, reviews, analytics, verification, documents, staff access, and operational settings.

The workspace is part of /workspace. It is not a separate website and does not require a separate account from the user's ordinary Massage Nexus account.

================================================================================
1. PRIMARY GOALS
================================================================================

The Operator Workspace should:

- allow authorized establishment representatives to manage official information;
- support one establishment or several branches;
- support owners, managers, staff, and agencies through permissions;
- separate provider-controlled information from community and editorial information;
- preserve edit history and sources;
- protect private business documents;
- support multilingual profile content;
- support service, price, therapist, and availability management;
- support secure media visibility;
- support booking and inquiry operations when available;
- provide analytics and task reminders;
- prevent one operator from accessing another establishment without permission.

================================================================================

================================================================================
35. CURRENT WORKING DECISIONS
================================================================================

- Operator Workspace is the accepted workspace name, and Operator is its broad functional role.
- The workspace supports owners, managers, and authorized staff.
- It is part of the single /workspace system.
- Establishment-specific permission is required.
- The establishment controls official provider information, not independent reviews or universal Service identity.
- establishment_service stores exact provider menu items.
- Therapist universal identity remains separate from establishment affiliation.
- Private documents and restricted media remain server-protected.
- Direct edits still create revision and audit history.
- Booking and commerce may be introduced in stages.
