DOCUMENT DESCRIPTION
--------------------
This document defines the private workspace used by sellers represented in Massage Nexus.

The Seller Workspace supports seller identity, product offers, prices, availability, fulfillment, media, promotions, reviews, verification, analytics, documents, staff permissions, orders, and future commerce functions.

A Product Profile represents a stable commercial product identity. A Seller Offer represents that seller's price, availability, condition, fulfillment, and commercial terms.

================================================================================
1. PRIMARY GOALS
================================================================================

The Seller Workspace should:

- let authorized sellers manage their public seller information;
- connect seller offers to normalized Product records;
- preserve Product identity separately from seller-specific information;
- support several stores, branches, channels, or warehouses;
- support price and availability updates;
- support media, promotions, and analytics;
- support verification and private documents;
- support staff permissions;
- prevent sellers from changing independent Product reviews or universal Product identity without review;
- support future checkout without requiring full commerce in the first release.

9. PRODUCT MAPPING
================================================================================

When a seller lists an offer, the system should:

1. search for an existing Product;
2. select the correct version and variant;
3. connect the Seller Offer;
4. propose a new Product only when no matching identity exists;
5. submit uncertain mappings for review.

AI may suggest a match, but should not create duplicate Products automatically without validation.

40. CURRENT WORKING DECISIONS
================================================================================

- Seller Workspace is part of /workspace.
- Product identity remains separate from Seller Offer.
- A seller may propose a Product but does not automatically control the universal Product record.
- Seller prices and stock remain seller-specific.
- Authenticity status requires evidence and controlled review.
- Advertising does not alter organic Product ratings or verification.
- Staff access is permission-based.
- Private business and payout documents remain protected.
- Full commerce may be introduced after Product discovery is stable.
- AI may suggest mappings but may not automatically create duplicate Products.
