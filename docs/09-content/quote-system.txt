================================================================================
1. PURPOSE AND BOUNDARY
================================================================================

Quotes give the homepage and other approved placements a calm editorial touch between more
functional sections. A quote is a short approved text with attribution, a category, and an
optional display window. It is curated platform content, not user-submitted content, and it does
not use article_main, article_body, or any user-contribution workflow.

Quotes are display-only. Users do not answer, vote on, or complete a quote, so quotes do not use
the completion-rotation behavior defined for Surveys and Quizzes. Quote rotation is time-based.

================================================================================
2. CONTENT AND ATTRIBUTION
================================================================================

Each quote should preserve:
- the quote text, multilingual where translated;
- attribution as a person, work, Anonymous, Traditional or Proverb, or Attribution Unverified;
- source context where known;
- category from the type_quote_category taxonomy;
- optional display window for seasonal or campaign use;
- review and lifecycle status.

Rules:
- do not invent attributions or attach a famous name to an unverified quote;
- prefer wellness, relaxation, massage, rest, self-care, and motivational themes fitting the
  platform voice;
- a quote with unresolved attribution doubt should remain unpublished rather than published with
  a guessed source;
- translated quote text follows docs/13-localization/translation-system.txt; the original language remains preserved in language_original_id. When a quote lacks a translation in the visitor's UI locale, fallback logic presents the original language or English fallback before hiding the module.

Anonymous means the creator intentionally published without a name or cannot be identified after
reasonable verification. Traditional or Proverb means the text belongs to a documented cultural
or oral tradition rather than one named speaker. Attribution Unverified means a claimed source is
not sufficiently supported and blocks publication. These states must not be collapsed into one
public "Unknown/Anonymous" label.

Rights and Source Approval
--------------------------

Attribution does not by itself authorize republication. Before approval, every quote must record
one supported publication basis:

- original Massage Nexus text;
- public-domain text;
- licensed text;
- permission obtained from the applicable rights holder;
- a short quotation or excerpt approved under the applicable legal-review standard.

Editorial verification should preserve the source edition, recording, interview, speech, or
other exact context; page, timestamp, or location where practical; source URL or identifier;
verification date; reviewer; and publication-right basis. A source title or URL may remain
private when necessary, but the supporting evidence must exist and be accessible to authorized
reviewers. A quote with uncertain attribution, uncertain wording, or unresolved rights must not
enter the approved rotation pool.

================================================================================
3. PLACEMENT AND ROTATION
================================================================================

Current placement is the homepage right sidebar (Quote of the Day). Future approved placements
may include Relaxation Rooms, Campus pages, or newsletters without changing quote ownership.

Users can hide the right sidebar module if they wish not to see it, and this preference will be saved in their settings.

Rotation is once a day from the approved active pool, respecting display windows. The daily
selection is global for the active locale rather than personalized per user. The platform timezone
defined by deployment configuration controls the calendar-day boundary; public behavior must not
depend on an editor's browser timezone.

The selector should be deterministic for one locale and calendar day, avoid immediate repeats
when at least two eligible quotes exist, and remain stable during the day even when a user reloads.
If the locale-specific pool is empty, apply the Translation System fallback to an eligible source
quote. If no eligible quote exists in any permitted fallback locale, hide the module rather than
display an unapproved, expired, restricted, or hard-coded quotation.

Rotation does not depend on per-user completion state because there is nothing for the user to
complete. Hiding the module is a user display preference and does not alter the global selection.

================================================================================
4. STORAGE AND ADMINISTRATION
================================================================================

The proposed collection is quote_main; see the structure guide at
data/structure_guide/quote_main.php. Quote categories come from
type_quote_category in data/taxonomy/massage_nexus/content_classification.json.
Before dynamic rotation is released, the structure guide and runtime form must represent the
source-verification and publication-right basis required by section 2; source_title and source_url
alone are not sufficient approval evidence.

Administration (attribution, source, language, category, display window, duplication review,
approval, scheduling, retirement, correction) belongs to the Admin Workspace editorial area per
docs/11-workspaces/admin-workspace.txt. The homepage currently renders one temporary hard-coded
sample quote; replacing it with quote_main-backed rotation is pending implementation.

The shared status_review taxonomy in
data/taxonomy/shared/record_lifecycle_and_review.json is the canonical review-status vocabulary.
The existing quote_main guide and runtime enum still use the older P/A/N/R codes; they require a
coordinated structure, code, and data migration before dynamic rotation is released.

A material wording or attribution correction must preserve an audit record and remove the
incorrect version from rotation immediately. Do not silently rewrite a previously displayed
quotation when the correction changes its wording, speaker, source, or publication-right basis.
Archived or withdrawn quotes remain unavailable to rotation while their authorized history is
preserved under the retention policy.

================================================================================
5. AI USE
================================================================================

AI may assist with:
- surfacing candidate quotes and possible sources for editorial review;
- suggesting a fitting category from type_quote_category;
- flagging likely duplicate or near-duplicate quotes already in the pool;
- drafting translation candidates for editorial and specialist review;
- identifying wording that may indicate misattribution or uncertain sourcing.

AI must not:
- invent, assume, or guess an attribution, source, or publication-right basis;
- attach a well-known name to an unverified quote;
- approve a quote or move it into the active rotation pool;
- silently alter a previously displayed quote's wording, speaker, or source;
- present a translation as approved before required review.

AI output remains a research and drafting aid. Attribution, publication-right basis,
category, and rotation eligibility still require the human review described in section 4.
