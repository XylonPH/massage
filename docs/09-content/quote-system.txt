================================================================================
1. PURPOSE AND BOUNDARY
================================================================================

Quotes give the homepage and other approved placements a calm editorial touch between more
functional sections. A quote is a short approved text with attribution, a category, and an
optional display window. It is curated platform content, not user-submitted content, and it does
not use article_main, article_body, or any user-contribution workflow.

Quotes are display-only. Users do not answer, vote on, or complete a quote, so quotes do not use
the completion-rotation behavior defined for Surveys and Quizzes. Quote rotation is time-based.

================================================================================
2. CONTENT AND ATTRIBUTION
================================================================================

Each quote should preserve:
- the quote text, multilingual where translated;
- attribution (person, work, tradition, or Unknown/Anonymous where genuinely unattributable);
- source context where known;
- category from the type_quote_category taxonomy;
- optional display window for seasonal or campaign use;
- review and lifecycle status.

Rules:
- do not invent attributions or attach a famous name to an unverified quote;
- prefer wellness, relaxation, massage, rest, self-care, and motivational themes fitting the
  platform voice;
- a quote with unresolved attribution doubt should remain unpublished rather than published with
  a guessed source;
- translated quote text follows the Translation System; the original language remains preserved.
- currently, quotes are in English only; whether to translate or use language-specific quotes is pending future decision.

================================================================================
3. PLACEMENT AND ROTATION
================================================================================

Current placement is the homepage right sidebar (Quote of the Day). Future approved placements
may include Relaxation Rooms, Campus pages, or newsletters without changing quote ownership.

Users can hide the right sidebar module if they wish not to see it, and this preference will be saved in their settings.

Rotation is once a day from the approved active pool, respecting display
windows. Rotation does not depend on per-user completion state because there is nothing for the
user to complete.

================================================================================
4. STORAGE AND ADMINISTRATION
================================================================================

The proposed collection is quote_main; see the structure guide at
data/structure_guide/quote_main.php. Quote categories come from
type_quote_category in data/taxonomy/massage_nexus/content_classification.json.

Administration (attribution, source, language, category, display window, duplication review,
approval, scheduling, retirement, correction) belongs to the Admin Workspace editorial area per
docs/11-workspaces/admin-workspace.txt. The homepage currently renders one temporary hard-coded
sample quote; replacing it with quote_main-backed rotation is pending implementation.

================================================================================
5. OPEN DECISIONS
