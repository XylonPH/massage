DOCUMENT DESCRIPTION
--------------------
This document defines the working marketing and social media system for Massage Nexus.

It covers brand communication, social accounts, content planning, campaigns, platform announcements, organic posts, paid promotion, partnerships, community interaction, content approval, publishing, analytics, archive, accessibility, security, and AI assistance.

Marketing explains and promotes Massage Nexus. It must not secretly control organic ratings, verification, moderation, reviews, or editorial conclusions.

================================================================================
1. PRIMARY GOALS
================================================================================

The Marketing and Social Media System should:

- make Massage Nexus recognizable;
- explain what the platform does;
- attract users, contributors, establishments, therapists, sellers, advertisers, and partners;
- direct audiences toward useful website pages;
- support launches, campaigns, events, articles, comics, profiles, and promotions;
- preserve a consistent visual and editorial identity;
- separate organic, editorial, sponsored, and official announcements;
- support several languages and cultural contexts;
- measure results without collecting unnecessary private data;
- prevent account loss, impersonation, and unsafe posting;
- keep reusable assets and post history organized;
- allow AI assistance without allowing uncontrolled automatic publishing.

================================================================================

- Massage Nexus as a platform;

Marketing must not:

- purchase or fabricate ratings;
- hide valid negative reviews;
- claim verification that does not exist;
- present paid placement as organic ranking;
- invent user activity;
- invent business information;
- expose private contact or identity information;
- imply that Massage Nexus directly operates a spa when it does not;
- imply that fictional characters are real employees or providers;
- publish serious accusations without editorial and legal review;
- use copyrighted media without permission.

1. POSITIONING
================================================================================

Massage Nexus is a massage, spa, wellness, and bodywork discovery platform designed to provide
richer, more current, more structured, and more transparent information than ordinary map
listings or generic search results.

Primary positioning:
Find your next great massage.

Differentiators:
- detailed service and provider information;
- field research;
- establishments missing from major maps;
- source and freshness information;
- reviews and lived experiences;
- multilingual access;
- provider and therapist profiles;
- comparison;
- community contributions;
- booking and operational features later;
- Massage Nexus original articles, comics, and media.

Marketing should not promise complete global coverage before it exists.
