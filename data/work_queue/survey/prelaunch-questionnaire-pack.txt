============================================

Purpose: English source copy for four Microsoft Forms and matching printed questionnaires.

Source specification: docs/08-community/survey-system.txt

Shared opening text
-------------------

Massage Nexus is being designed as a directory, information, booking, community, and learning
platform for massage and related wellness services. This questionnaire asks what would make the
platform useful and trustworthy for you. It takes about 7 to 10 minutes. Participation is voluntary.
You may skip any optional question or stop before submitting. This initial form is in English.
Responses will be analyzed in aggregate to guide product decisions. Do not enter names, telephone
numbers, exact addresses, medical histories, or confidential business information in comment boxes.

Consent question used in every form:
CONSENT-01 | Required | Single choice
I am at least 18 years old, I have read the explanation above, and I voluntarily agree to take part.
Options: Yes, continue [YES] | No, end the form [NO]
Branching: NO -> End of form.

Shared closing text
-------------------

Thank you. Your response will be combined with other responses. If you want to volunteer for a
separate follow-up conversation, use a separate contact form so your contact details are not linked
automatically to this questionnaire.
